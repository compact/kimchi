Namespace: time

KIMCHI. time

Time controller. When the time is on, the Bodies move; when the time is off, the Bodies stop moving. We use "on"/"off"/"isOn" rather than "start"/"stop"/"isEnabled" for a better adjective and to avoid confusion with THREE.Clock.
